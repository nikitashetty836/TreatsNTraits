Basic terms to know for this game
-Offspring: possible children that can be created from the parent

-Traits: characteristcs that can be passed from parent to offspring
		Examples of heritable: Eye color, hair color
		Non examples: personality, likes, dislikes
		
-Genes: small section of a DNA that codes for a certain protein
		instructions for proteins which help determine your traits
		genes are NOT the physical 
		
-Allele: different forms of a certain gene (i.e. different alleles = different traits)
